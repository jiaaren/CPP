### Operator overload
#### Notations
- Types (all the same)
    1. **In**fix notation - `1 + 1`
    2. **Pre**fix notation - `+ 1 1`
    3. **Post**fix notation - `1 1 +`

- Prefix notation (aka functional notation)
    - `+ 1 1` = `+(1, 1)`

- Infix notation
  - `1 + 1` = `1, +( 1 )`
